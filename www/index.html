Remote Virus Protection

Scanning files with multiple virus scanners is a crucial security measure for everyone. Relying on a single antivirus solution is not enough in today's threat landscape, as new types of viruses and malware are emerging all the time. By scanning files with multiple scanners, you can increase the likelihood of detecting and blocking malicious content, thereby protecting your systems and the systems of your users.

Fortunately, integrating the RVP service into your applications is a breeze, thanks to its easy-to-use HTTP interface. The service is highly versatile and can be easily implemented in almost any application, providing users with an extra layer of security. To take your security to the next level, you can even contribute to the project on Github and help make it even more effective and secure.
